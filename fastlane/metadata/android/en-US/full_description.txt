<p><i>Bluesky Redirect</i> is a simple app for automatically launching Bluesky links in your preferred Bluesky client. Currently, most social app forks are supported, and we welcome pull requests.</p><p>Once you download the app, you can choose your client app and then enable all supported domains, so that when you tap a recognized link, it gets passed to <i>Bluesky Redirect</i>.</p>

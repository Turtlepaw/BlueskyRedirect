<p><i>Lemmy Redirect</i> is a simple app for automatically launching fediverse links in your preferred Lemmy client. Currently, most domains from the <a href="https://lemmyverse.net/communities" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Lemmy Explorer</a> are supported.</p><p><i>Lemmy Redirect</i> supports most active and alive instances, but excludes dead instances and instances that haven't had any activity recently. This is to keep the list as short as possible and avoid crashes.</p><p><i>Lemmy Redirect</i> currently supports the following clients:</p><ul><li><a href='https://codeberg.org/Bazsalanszky/Eternity' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Eternity</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/dessalines/jerboa" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Jerboa</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/liftoff-app/liftoff" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Liftoff</a></li><li><a href="https://lemmy.world/c/summit" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Summit</a></li><li><a href='https://github.com/laurencedawson/sync-for-lemmy' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Sync</a></li><li><a href='https://github.com/thunder-app/thunder' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Thunder</a></li></ul><p>And the following auto-discovery clients:</p><ul><li><a href='https://github.com/diegoberaldin/RaccoonForLemmy' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Raccoon</a></li></ul><p>Once you download the app, you can choose your client app and then enable all supported domains, so that when you tap a recognized link, it gets passed to <i>Lemmy Redirect</i>.</p>

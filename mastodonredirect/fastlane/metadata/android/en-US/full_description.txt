<p><i>Mastodon Redirect</i> is a simple app for automatically launching fediverse links in your preferred Mastodon client. Currently, most domains on <a href="https://instances.social/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">instances.social</a> are supported.</p><p><i>Mastodon Redirect</i> supports most active and alive instances, but excludes dead instances and instances that haven't had any activity recently. This is to keep the list as short as possible and avoid crashes.</p><p>Mastodon Redirect currently supports the following clients:</p><ul><li><a href='https://github.com/elk-zone/elk' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Elk (PWA: Stable or Canary)</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/caraesten/Fedilab" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Fedilab (F-DROID or Play Store)</a></li><li><a href='https://github.com/mastodon/mastodon-android' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Mastodon</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/sk22/megalodon" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Megalodon</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/LucasGGamerM/moshidon" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Moshidon</a></li><li><a href='https://hachyderm.io/@phanpy' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Phanpy (PWA: Stable or Dev)</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/tateisu/SubwayTooter" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Subway Tooter</a></li><li><a href="https://github.com/tooot-app/app" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Tooot</a></li><li><a href='https://mastodon.social/@trunksapp' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Trunks (Native or Web)</a></li></ul><p>Once you download the app, you can choose your client app and then enable all supported domains, so that when you tap a recognized link, it gets passed to <i>Mastodon Redirect</i>.</p>

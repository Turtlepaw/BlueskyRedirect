<p><i>PeerTube Redirect</i> is a simple app for automatically launching fediverse links in your preferred PeerTube client.</p><p><i>PeerTube Redirect</i> currently supports the following clients:</p><ul><li><a href='https://github.com/stom79/Fedilab' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Fedilab (F-Droid or Play Store)</a></li><li><a href='https://grayjay.app/' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Grayjay (Stable, Unstable, or Play Store)</a></li><li><a href='https://github.com/TeamNewPipe/NewPipe' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>NewPipe (Release or Debug-Main)</a></li></ul><p>Once you download the app, you can choose your client app and then enable all supported domains, so that when you tap a recognized link, it gets passed to <i>PeerTube Redirect</i>.</p></body> </html>
